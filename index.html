Corona Kavach and Corona Rakshak

This website is going to make you aware and explain in detail
about the Covid-19 special Health Insurance Policies that
the Indian Government has rolled out for its citizens

I already have a Health Insurance Policy, Why buy another one ?

  • Your existing Health Insurance Policy may not cover your hospitalization due to Corona

  • Even if the Policy covers the hospitalization, it won't pay for PPE and other consumable cost, which alone amounts to almost 25% of the total hospitalization bill. And then you have to bear that amount

  • Some hospitals don't accept general Health Insurance Policies, because they don't want the risk of the Health Insurance Provider denying the reimbursement

How to decide from which Health Insurance Company should I buy the Policy ?

It is actually bit simple in case of Corona Kavach to decide as the Policy is laid out by IRDAI and a Health Insurance Provider cannot change its terms. So now you may think that I should buy this Policy from a provider who will charge me the least premium. But I would highly advice you against that thought. Alongwith cost we also need to consider the service and network hospitals of the Health Insurance Providers.

What is Corona Rakshak ?

  • It is a benefit based Health Insurance Policy

  • Lump sum benefit equal to 100% of the Sum Insured shall be payable on positive diagnosis of COVID, requiring hospitalization for a minimum continuous period of 72 hours.

  • The positive diagnosis of COVID shall be from a government authorized diagnostic centre.
